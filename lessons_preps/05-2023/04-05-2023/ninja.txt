The Ninja Turtles are a group of four mutant turtles. They are trained by a rat sensei named Splinter.
The turtles are named after Renaissance artists: Leonardo, Michelangelo, Donatello, and Raphael. They live 
in the sewers of New York City. Their archenemy is the villainous Shredder. The turtles love eating pizza.
They fight to protect the city from evil. Each turtle has a unique weapon. Their catchphrase is "Cowabunga!"
The Ninja Turtles are beloved by fans worldwide.
